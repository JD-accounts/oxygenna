Chips represent complex entities in small blocks, such as a contact. They can contain a photo, short title string, and brief information.

Contact chips represent contact information that users have for people in a compact way. They are invoked and inserted into a text field (usually the To field) when the user starts typing a contact’s name, sees the contact’s addresses, and selects the correct one. Contact chips can be added directly to a text field from a menu of contacts.
